Welcome!


This is a fun website to help you visualise your history on TfL. Starting with a faded map, once you begin to add stations their names will light up. As you add more stations on a line, the segments in-between will become visible, too.


TfL let you export the last 8 weeks of journey data from their website. Simply log in and click on the button to export your jouney history as a CSV file. If you don't have an account, but have an Oyster/contactless card you use for journeys, just sign up and follow the steps to link them.


N.B.: Syncing between this device and others is currently unavailable. All data is stored in the browser's localStorage. Support for accounts and cross-device syncing will come in the near future.

Elizabeth line coming soon...


Due to the many changes on the new Tube Map, integrating the new Elizabeth line will take a lot of work! I anticipate this being a task for when the line is fully opened and the map will face no further changes.


For now you can click the roundal to change between the current map and the new branch.
